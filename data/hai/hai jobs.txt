# Hai Recovery and Rescue Jobs

# Easy ones
mission "Recovery Standard Hai Asset"
	name "Secure Hai Sensor Pods in <waypoint>"
	description "A stealth probe monitoring the Unfettered borders has been disabled in the <waypoint>. The Hai cannot risk sending an official military vessel. You are contracted to retrieve the ecological sensor logs weighting <cargo> and deliver them quietly to the spaceport in <destination>. Payment for successful extraction is 90,000 credits."
	job
	repeat
	"offer precedence" 1000
	source
		attributes "hai"
		not attributes "unfettered"
	waypoint
		attributes "unfettered"
	destination
		attributes "hai"
		not attributes "unfettered"
	cargo 10 20 0.7
	deadline
	to offer
		"hai_arc_rescue_completed" == 1

	npc
		system waypoint
		government "Derelict"
		personality "derelict" "staying"
		ship "Geochelone" "Disabled Stealth Probe"
		on assist
			"hai_asset_ambush_1_ready" = 1
			conversation
				"Your techs carefully detach the stealth sensor pods. As the data finishes transferring, proximity alarms blare—Unfettered patrols have noticed the energy signature of your extraction gear."
	on complete
		conversation
			"You land softly at the spaceport. A small group of Hai researchers quickly approaches, their fur ruffled with visible anxiety. They take the sensor pods and immediately begin checking the encryption seals."
			"'Thank you, Captain,' one whispers, eyes darting around the hangar. 'If our Unfettered cousins or your human authorities found this data, the consequences for our peaceful society would be devastating. Your discretion is deeply appreciated.'"
		"rr_operations_jobs_done" ++
		"rr_hai_jobs_done" ++
		"reputation: Hai" += 0.01
		"reputation: R&R Stranded Ships Operations" += 0.01
		payment 90000
	on fail
		conversation
			"A panicked Hai official contacts you over a heavily encrypted line. 'The data is lost? You do not understand what you have done. The Unfettered will use those logs to find our blind spots! Please, leave our space.'"
		"reputation: Hai" -= 0.15
		"reputation: R&R Stranded Ships Operations" -= 0.15
	on abort
		conversation
			"The Hai liaison shakes their head slowly on the monitor. 'You gave us your word, and then you abandoned the mission. Humans are as unpredictable as the Unfettered. We cannot rely on you.'"
		"reputation: Hai" -= 0.20
		"reputation: R&R Stranded Ships Operations" -= 0.20
